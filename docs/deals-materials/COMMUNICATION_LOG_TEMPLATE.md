## ğŸ“ Usage Instructions

### When to Log
- **Immediately** after every communication (within 1-2 hours max)
- Before memory fades and details are lost
- Even brief 2-minute WhatsApp exchanges should be logged

### What to Include
- **Context:** Why did this communication happen?
- **Key Points:** What was discussed? (bullet format)
- **Decisions:** Any agreements or choices made
- **Requests:** What client asked for or approved
- **Action Items:** Clear WHO + WHAT + WHEN
- **Tone/Sentiment:** Client satisfaction level (optional but helpful)

### Best Practices
- Use timestamps for accountability
- Quote exact requests when possible (copy-paste from WhatsApp)
- Note if client seems confused/frustrated/happy
