### Body Language Tips:
- Smile naturally (relatable, not salesy)
- Maintain eye contact (shows confidence)
- Open posture (no crossed arms)
- Use hand gestures (moderate, not excessive)
- Lean in slightly (shows interest/engagement)
- Mirror their energy (if they're excited, match it; if skeptical, stay calm)

### Voice Tips:
- Speak clearly (not too fast)
- Vary your tone (avoid monotone)
- Pause for emphasis (silence is powerful)
- Lower voice slightly (deeper = more authoritative)
- Avoid filler words ("um", "eh", "seperti")
