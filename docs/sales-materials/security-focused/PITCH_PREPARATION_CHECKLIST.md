## üìä SUCCESS METRICS

### Pitch is SUCCESSFUL if:

- [ ] Owner acknowledges the hack trauma (emotional hook works)
- [ ] Owner says "Saya paham" atau "Betul juga ya" (they get it)
- [ ] Owner asks about payment options (buying signal!)
- [ ] Owner requests time to think with specific deadline (soft yes)

### Pitch is NEUTRAL if:

- [ ] Owner interested but budget concern (follow up potential)
- [ ] Owner interested but timing concern (check back later)

### Pitch is FAILED if:
